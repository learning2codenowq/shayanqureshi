About Me

Shayan Qureshi

I started memorizing the Quran at 18 in Sharjah with no real structure, just repetition and hope. Progress was slow and frustrating. It wasn't until my teachers introduced me to proper memorization techniques that changed everything.

By 20, by the mercy of Allah, I had completed my Hifdh under two government-certified teachers recognized by the Government of Sharjah. I went on to give adhan and lead salah as a substitute Imam at my local masjid... something I never imagined when I first started.

I also hold an Ijazah in Al-Qa'idah Al-Madaniyyah, certified by Masjid An-Nabawi in Madinah, authorising me to teach the methodology of the Prophet's ï·º masjid.

I've taught children, teenagers, and busy adults across the world. Every class I teach is structured around your pace and goals with a clear plan, homework after every session, and notes so nothing gets lost.
